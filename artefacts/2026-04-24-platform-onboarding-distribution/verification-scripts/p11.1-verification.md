### Scenario 1: Discovery template contains three new attribution sections

**Covers:** AC1

**Steps:**
1. Open `.github/templates/discovery.md`.
2. Search (Ctrl+F / Cmd+F) for `## Contributors`.
3. Search for `## Reviewers`.
4. Search for `## Approved By` (or `## Approved-By`).
5. For each section found, read the HTML comment (grey text starting with `<!--`) immediately under the heading.

**Expected outcome:**
> All three sections are present in the file. Each has an HTML comment that explains: (a) what kind of content to put there, and (b) makes a distinction between engineering and non-engineering roles — for example, the `## Approved By` comment should mention that a non-engineering approver is expected (something like "Product Manager", "Business Analyst", or "Business Lead").
