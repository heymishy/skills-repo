## Coverage gaps

**AI-skill runtime gap:** AC1 and AC2 describe the output produced by the AI when it reads the SKILL.md instructions. Direct automated verification of AI output is not possible without a live model invocation. Unit tests verify the **instruction text encodes the required behaviour**. Manual verification scenarios confirm end-to-end output.

**Risk:** 🟡 Medium — mitigated by specific single-turn instruction text and the `single-turn-constraint` marker enforced by the contract.
