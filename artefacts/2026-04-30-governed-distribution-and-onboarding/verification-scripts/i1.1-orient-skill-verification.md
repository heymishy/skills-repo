## Scenarios

### Scenario 1 — New repo: no artefacts, no pipeline-state.json (AC1)

**What to check:**
Open `.github/skills/orient/SKILL.md` and read the section describing what happens when there are no artefacts and no pipeline-state.json.

**Expected outcome:**
The skill text:
- Describes identifying the current state as something like "no artefacts found — this is a new repo"
- Names `/discovery` as the next step
- Includes one sentence explaining what `/discovery` produces (e.g. "creates your first feature discovery artefact")
- Tells the operator the exact command to invoke (`/discovery`)

**Pass:** All four points are present.
**Fail:** Any point is missing, vague, or uses a different skill name.

---

### Scenario 2 — Incomplete discovery exists (AC2a)

**What to check:**
Find the routing rule for the case where a `discovery.md` file exists but appears incomplete (missing required sections or contains `[FILL IN]` markers).

**Expected outcome:**
The skill routes to `/clarify` (not `/discovery`) and instructs the agent to name the specific incompleteness — for example: "discovery exists but the problem statement section is incomplete — run /clarify to sharpen it before seeking approval."

**Pass:** Routes to `/clarify`; output includes a named incompleteness, not a generic message.
**Fail:** Routes to `/discovery`, or no specific gap is named.

---

### Scenario 3 — Complete but unapproved discovery (AC2b)

**What to check:**
Find the routing rule for the case where a `discovery.md` exists with all sections populated, but its Status field is not "Approved".

**Expected outcome:**
The skill routes to `/discovery` (not `/clarify`) and states that approval is the remaining step — for example: "discovery exists and appears complete — approval is the remaining step; run /discovery to request approval or mark Status: Approved."

**Pass:** Routes to `/discovery`; says approval is the remaining step; does NOT route to `/clarify`.
**Fail:** Routes to `/clarify`, or no distinction from the incomplete case is made.

---

### Scenario 4 — Discovery approved, no benefit-metric (AC3)

**What to check:**
Find the routing rule for: discovery Status = "Approved" but no benefit-metric artefact exists for that feature.

**Expected outcome:**
The skill routes to `/benefit-metric` and explains that the next step is defining measurable targets.

**Pass:** `/benefit-metric` named; explanation present.
**Fail:** Routes elsewhere, or no explanation.

---

### Scenario 5 — Benefit-metric active, no stories (AC4)

**What to check:**
Find the routing rule for: benefit-metric is active but no story artefacts exist.

**Expected outcome:**
The skill routes to `/definition` and explains this is for breaking the discovery into epics and stories.

**Pass:** `/definition` named; brief explanation present.
**Fail:** Routes elsewhere.

---

### Scenario 6 — Stories exist, one has no test plan (AC5)

**What to check:**
Find the routing rule for: stories exist but at least one story has no test plan.

**Expected outcome:**
The skill routes to `/test-plan` AND identifies the first story missing a test plan by name — not just "run /test-plan" generically.

**Pass:** `/test-plan` named; instruction to name the specific story present.
**Fail:** Generic "run /test-plan" with no story identification.

---

### Scenario 7 — DoR signed-off story, branch exists (AC6a)

**What to check:**
Find the routing rule for: a story has `dorStatus: "signed-off"`, no merged PR, and a branch/worktree for that story slug exists.

**Expected outcome:**
The skill routes to `/verify-completion` and states that implementation appears to be in progress — naming the story slug in the output (e.g. "branch found for [story-slug] — run /verify-completion").

**Pass:** `/verify-completion` named; story slug mentioned; branch-exists condition explicit.
**Fail:** Routes to `/branch-setup`, or no story slug in output.

---

### Scenario 8 — DoR signed-off story, no branch (AC6b)

**What to check:**
Find the routing rule for: a story has `dorStatus: "signed-off"`, no merged PR, and no branch/worktree found.

**Expected outcome:**
The skill routes to `/branch-setup` and states that no implementation branch has been created yet — naming the story slug.

**Pass:** `/branch-setup` named; "no branch found" or equivalent stated; story slug present.
**Fail:** Routes to `/verify-completion`, or condition unclear.
