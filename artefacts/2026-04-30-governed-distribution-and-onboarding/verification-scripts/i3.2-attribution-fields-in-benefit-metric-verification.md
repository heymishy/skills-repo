### Scenario 2: The skill warns the operator and offers two options when Approved By is empty

**Covers:** AC2

**Steps:**
1. In `.github/skills/benefit-metric/SKILL.md`, find the part that describes what happens when `Approved By` is empty or contains a placeholder.
2. Read the warning instruction.

**Expected outcome:**
> When `Approved By` is empty or contains a placeholder like "Pending", the skill displays a warning. The warning offers two explicit options:
>
> - **Option (a):** Stop — go back to the discovery artefact, populate `Approved By`, and re-run `/benefit-metric`
> - **Option (b):** Proceed — acknowledge the gap now and continue writing the benefit-metric artefact; a note will be added to the produced artefact
>
> Both options are clearly labelled. The operator is not left guessing what to do.

**Result:** [ ] Pass  [ ] Fail
