### Scenario 1: Discovery SKILL.md includes an Attribution section with the three required sub-fields

**Covers:** AC1

**Steps:**
1. Open `.github/skills/discovery/SKILL.md`.
2. Search the document for `## Attribution` (or equivalent section heading).
3. Read the sub-fields listed in that section.

**Expected outcome:**
> An `## Attribution` section is present. It contains three sub-fields:
> - **Contributors** — lists the names and roles of people who shaped the discovery
> - **Reviewers** — lists who reviewed and gave input
> - **Approved By** — the name, role, and date of the person who approved the discovery for pipeline entry
>
> All three sub-fields are present. None are missing.
