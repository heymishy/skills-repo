# DoR Contract: i3.1 — Attribution fields in /discovery SKILL.md

**Feature:** 2026-04-30-governed-distribution-and-onboarding
**Story:** i3.1-attribution-fields-in-discovery
**Date:** 2026-04-30

---

## What will be built

Edit `.github/skills/discovery/SKILL.md` to add:

1. A `## Attribution` section in the artefact output template block with three sub-fields:
   - `Contributors` — one or more names and roles
   - `Reviewers` — one or more names and roles
   - `Approved By` — in `Name — Role — Date` format, or "Pending"
2. Instruction in Step 1 to prompt for contributor names and roles before finalising the artefact
3. Reminder in the completion/closing step: when `Approved By` is empty or "Pending", prompt the operator to fill it before setting discovery status to "Approved"
4. Format specification: `Name — Role — Date` is the required format for an attribution-complete `Approved By` entry

## What will NOT be built

- Automated validation of `Approved By` content (role classification is H-GOV's job in i3.3)
- Attribution fields on any artefact type other than `discovery.md`
- Changes to `pipeline-state.schema.json`
- Changes to `.github/templates/discovery.md` (template changes are out of scope)
- Retroactive modification of existing discovery artefacts
