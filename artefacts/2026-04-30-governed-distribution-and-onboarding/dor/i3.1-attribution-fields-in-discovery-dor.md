## Contract Proposal — i3.1

**What will be built:**
The `.github/skills/discovery/SKILL.md` file will be edited to add:
1. A new `## Attribution` section in the output artefact template block, containing three sub-fields: `Contributors` (one or more names and roles), `Reviewers` (one or more names and roles), and `Approved By` (name — role — date, or "Pending")
2. An instruction in the completion/closing step of the skill to remind the operator when `Approved By` is still empty or "Pending"
3. An instruction in Step 1 of the skill to prompt for contributor names and roles before finalising the artefact
4. A statement in the template that `Name — Role — Date` is the required format for an attribution-complete `Approved By` entry

**What will NOT be built:**
- Changes to any artefact other than `discovery/SKILL.md`
- Automated validation of the `Approved By` content (role classification)
- Attribution fields on benefit-metric or other artefact types (i3.2 scope)
- Any changes to `pipeline-state.schema.json`
- Retroactive modification of existing discovery artefacts

**How each AC will be verified:**
