## Setup

**Before you start:**
1. Have a feature with at least one story that has upstream dependencies in its Dependencies block.
2. Have a separate story with no dependencies (Dependencies: None).
3. Have a story with at least one NFR declared.
4. Confirm `pipeline-state.schema.json` is readable from the repo root.

**Reset between scenarios:** Each scenario uses a separate story fixture and does not share state.

### Scenario 1: /definition-of-ready prompts for schemaDepends when a story has upstream dependencies

**Covers:** AC1

**Steps:**
1. Run `/definition-of-ready` on a story that has an upstream dependency listed in its Dependencies block.
2. Observe the H-block checks being run.

**Expected outcome:**
> Before the DoR contract is signed off, the skill asks: "Which pipeline-state.json schema fields does this story expect to be written by [upstream story slug]?" and adds a `schemaDepends: [...]` entry to the DoR contract.
