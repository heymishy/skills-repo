## Scenarios

### 🔴 Scenario 1: The board visually matches the product-view redesign (light mode)

**Covers:** AC2

**Steps:**
1. Open the product-level kanban board in light mode.
2. Open the published product-view redesign mockup in another tab.
3. Compare: the page background colour, the card/column background colour, the main text colour, and the accent colour (used for links/highlights).

**Expected outcome:** All four of these — page background, card background, text colour, accent colour — look like the same palette as the mockup, not a different, older-looking colour scheme. If any of these four looks visibly different (e.g. the board's background is a different shade of grey, or the accent colour is a different blue), record it as a fail with a note on which specific element didn't match.

**Negative check:** The board should NOT still show plain grey borders, default black text, or a generic blue link colour that doesn't match the mockup's specific palette.

### 🟡 Scenario 2: Dark mode still looks legible and on-brand

**Covers:** AC3 (visual part)

**Steps:**
1. Switch to dark mode (via the theme toggle) on the same kanban board.
2. Look at the column headers, card text, and the green/amber/red health indicators on cards.

**Expected outcome:** Everything remains easily readable (text isn't low-contrast-on-dark), and the health indicator colours (green/amber/red) still look like green/amber/red, just adapted for a dark background — not washed out or illegible.

**Negative check:** Text should not appear as dark-grey-on-dark-background (illegible), and health-indicator colours should not disappear or look identical to each other.

**Pass / Fail:** ___
