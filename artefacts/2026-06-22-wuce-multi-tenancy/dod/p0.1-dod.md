## DoD Observations

1. AC11 specified `isOwner` as a public export. The implementation internalized ownership checking inside `requireJourneyAccess()` and exposed `POLICY` as the policy selector instead. This is a better API design: callers declare intent (`POLICY.TENANT` vs `POLICY.OWNER`) rather than calling a predicate and writing their own branch. The story was written before the final API design was settled. **Improve candidate:** update story templates to defer export surface definition to the implementation plan step rather than the story AC — export lists in ACs often lag the actual design.

2. Test count exceeded plan by 4 (16 vs 12). The extras were edge-case variants (undefined vs null, both policy variants for UNAUTHENTICATED). Positive signal — no gaps.
