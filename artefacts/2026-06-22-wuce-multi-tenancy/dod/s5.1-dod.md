## RISK-ACCEPT: AC2/AC3 simulation not run in production

**Decision:** PG and Redis failure simulation (temporarily revoking DATABASE_URL or Upstash credentials) was not executed in production. Rationale: the error handlers (`_pgWrite()` catch and `persistSession()` catch) are verified by code inspection to emit the correct log patterns, and the risk of triggering a real production outage (potentially losing in-flight journal data) is disproportionate at beta scale with one active user. The handlers are defensive paths that cannot be practically unit-tested against live managed services. Log pattern correctness was validated by inspection; a future stress-test run can confirm end-to-end.
