## Findings

**1-L1 (LOW):** AC6 embeds a testing implementation hint — "using a synthetic fixture with artificial delay if needed." The testable outcome is the completedAt delta greater than 5ms on any hardware. The fixture approach is one possible testing strategy, but including it in the AC conflates specification with implementation. This does not block the story but reduces AC clarity. Recommendation: strip the clause to "then the `completedAt` timestamp in the trace is greater than `startedAt` by at least 5ms on any hardware." No block.
