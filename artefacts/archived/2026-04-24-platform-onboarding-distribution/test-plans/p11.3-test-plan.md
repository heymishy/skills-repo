## Coverage gaps

**Partial gap — AI-skill runtime:** AC1–AC4 describe behaviours that are implemented as AI instruction text in `/definition-of-ready` SKILL.md, not as runnable code. Direct programmatic testing of AI decision logic is not possible without a live model invocation. Unit tests verify the **required instruction text is present** (the necessary precondition for the behaviour to occur) and that the instruction text is precise enough to encode each AC. A `/verify-completion` step with a manual scenario run is required to confirm end-to-end behaviour.

**Risk:** 🟡 Medium — the instruction text may be present but the AI may not apply it consistently. Accepted under WS0.6 (AI skill accuracy is out of scope for automated tests). The manual verification scenarios below provide the human signal gate.
