There is no second squad in Phase 1 dogfood context, making this AC unverifiable at P1.8 DoD. A story AC that cannot be verified at closure is a definition gap — the DoD cannot be signed off against an AC that has not and cannot be tested.

**Resolution options (no story rework required — AC can be annotated):**
1. Add a note to AC5: "Verified post-DoD at first non-dogfood onboarding event — not a Phase 1 gate AC." and record in decisions.md as accepted for Phase 1.
2. Move AC5 to adoption checklist / onboarding guide documentation rather than the story AC set, and reference it from P1.8's out-of-scope or NFR section as a post-Phase-1 adoption requirement.

Either option preserves P1.8's intent without changing the deliverable or blocking DoR.
