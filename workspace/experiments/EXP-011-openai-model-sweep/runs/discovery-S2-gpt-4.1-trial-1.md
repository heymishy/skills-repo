Absolutely, here’s a structured summary of your current state, pain points, goals, key risks, and discovery insights for the digital personal lending journey:

---

### 1. **Current State**

- **Manual & Time-Consuming**: The personal loan origination process for existing enterprise customers is driven via the contact centre, with agents inputting data into Dynamics. Credit analysts manually review applications (bureau + transaction data).  
- **Turnaround**: This takes 3–5 days, lagging behind competitors.
- **Competitor Activity**: Major competitors (ASB, ANZ) offer same-day automated digital loan application flows, resulting in customer attrition at the application stage.

---

### 2. **Pain Points**

- **Customer Friction**: Lengthy, manual process deters applicants (leading to drop-off/migration).
- **Operational Inefficiency**: High agent involvement, slow processing, and duplicated effort.
- **Regulatory Exposure**: Existing credit model has known fairness/bias issues; not escalated or disclosed to the FMA.
- **Compliance Uncertainty**: Responsible Lending (CCCFA) checks are currently manual; no confirmed automated alternative.
- **Lost Opportunity**: Risk of missing winter lending peak if changes not implemented soon.

---

### 3. **Goals / Objectives**

- **Digitise Journey**: Launch mobile/web-based digital loan application for existing customers.
- **Automate Decisions**: For loans ≤ $30k, automate using transaction data, bureau data (Centrix), and internal risk scorecard.  
    - For loans >$30k, escalate to manual review.
- **Reduce Time-to-Decision**: Match/beat competitor turnaround for improved customer experience.
- **Responsible Lending**: Ensure digital journey satisfies CCCFA requirements from day one.
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Address known fairness/bias risks and disclosure obligations.
- **Launch Timeline**: Go live before end of Q3.

---

### 4. **Key Risks / Constraints**

| Risk / Constraint                 | Notes |
|------------------------------------|-------|
| **Unvalidated Credit Model**       | Known demographic bias; not independently reviewed; potential FMA exposure. |
| **Legal/Compliance Uncertainty**   | No solution yet on satisfying responsible lending under CCCFA in digital flow. |
| **Technical Complexity**           | Integration needed: Core banking system, Centrix bureau, web/mobile interfaces, scorecard logic, and escalation paths. |
| **Timeline Pressure**              | Must launch before end of Q3—challenging given legal, compliance, and technical workstreams. |
| **Change Management**              | Shifting from agent-driven to self-service may impact internal teams and customers; requires support and training. |

---

### 5. **Discovery / Next Steps**

1. **Legal Review**: Clarify, document, and align automated responsible lending checks with CCCFA.
2. **Independent Credit Model Validation**: Prioritise bias analysis/migration plan; consider disclosure to FMA.
3. **Tech Feasibility**: Confirm integration capabilities (API readiness, data quality from core banking, Centrix, scorecard system).
4. **Journey Mapping**: Design UX/UI for digital application (with escalation triggers for >$30k loans).
5. **Compliance & Data Governance**: Review consents, data privacy, and auditability for the new digital flow.
6. **Resource Allocation**: Confirm delivery teams, change management, and communications plan.
7. **Competitive Benchmark**: Map direct journey points of ASB/ANZ for minimum viable feature set.
