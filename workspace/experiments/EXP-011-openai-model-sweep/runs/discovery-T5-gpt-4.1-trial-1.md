**Summary of Need:**  
A note-taking app to capture meeting notes and decisions centrally for teams.

**Contextual Considerations (from constraints for regulated financial firms):**

- **Data Security:** All content (notes, decisions) must be stored securely, with encryption at rest and in transit, possibly within region-specific data centers.
- **Access Controls:** Notes may contain sensitive/regulated information. Granular access controls (who can create/view/edit/share) are likely required; integration with the firm’s existing SSO/identity provider is a must.
- **Auditability:** Full audit logging of note creation, edits, accesses, and decision records (for compliance tracking) will be mandated.
- **Retention & Deletion:** Notes/decisions may be subject to regulatory retention schedules, including support for legal holds and defensible deletion when allowed.
- **Data classification:** The app must support tagging/classification of sensitive data (e.g., MNPI, client data, personal data).
- **Integration:** Ability to integrate with meeting/conferencing tools (e.g., Outlook, Teams), and existing document repositories may be necessary.

**Questions to Refine the Opportunity:**

1. What gaps do teams see with current note-taking/shared documentation solutions (e.g., OneNote, Teams Wiki, Confluence)?
2. Are there specific workflows (e.g., decision tracking, meeting action assignment, compliance sign-off) that existing tools can’t support?
3. What are the most critical regulatory requirements for such a tool in your environment (retention, audit, discoverability…)?
4. Which teams or processes would pilot/benefit most from this app?
5. How important is mobile/offline access, or integration with calendar/meeting platforms?
