### Competitive Analysis
- **Value Proposition**: Ensure that the proposed feature clearly differentiates itself from competitors like Wise in terms of speed, cost, and convenience. Highlight the two-hour settlement time and reduced transaction fees.
  
### Technical Implementation
- **Infrastructure Alignment**: Work closely with your Australian counterpart to ensure technical alignment of systems and processes, focusing on the internal routing channel implementation.
- **Integration**: Seamlessly integrate this new feature into the existing payment and customer interface systems. Consider any dependencies on current treasury systems.
- **Scalability**: Build the system to be scalable, allowing easy adaptation to a potential AU-to-NZ phase in the future.

### Compliance and Regulatory
- **AML/CFT Screening**: Extend existing AML/CFT protocols to include the new channel, ensuring compliance with both NZ and AU regulatory requirements.
- **Regulations Notifications**: Prepare and submit necessary regulatory documentation and notifications well in advance of the pilot.

### Pricing Strategy
- **Pricing Model**: Develop a clear pricing strategy that undercuts existing solutions and fits customer expectations. Utilize data and customer feedback from the pilot to refine the model.

### Risk Management
- **Pilot Program**: Conduct a thorough pilot program to identify potential issues, including system performance and customer satisfaction.
- **Risk Evaluation**: Regularly assess risks related to system reliability, potential fraud, and regulatory challenges.

### Customer Experience
- **User Interface**: Ensure that the user interface clearly communicates the benefits and costs associated with the new payment feature.
- **Customer Support**: Equip customer service teams with training and resources to effectively handle inquiries and issues related to the new feature.

### Marketing and Communication
- **Targeted Promotion**: Develop marketing campaigns targeting customers with frequent trans-Tasman transactions, emphasizing the speed and cost benefits.
- **Feedback Loop**: Create mechanisms for collecting and analyzing customer feedback to continuously improve the service.
