# Benefit Metric: Automated Regulatory Reporting Pipeline

**Feature slug:** regulatory-reporting-pipeline-automation
**Status:** Active

## Primary metrics
- Cycle time reduction: compress monthly return preparation from 6–8 business days to 2 business days of analyst review and sign-off.
- Audit trail producibility: 100% of returns must be exportable within 5 business days.
- Submission deadline compliance: zero missed regulatory submission deadlines per quarter.

## Evidence plan
- Baseline: current manual return preparation and manual audit trail records.
- Measurement: monthly reporting cycle duration, audit trail export drill results, and submission deadline variance.
- Gate: before production activation of any normalisation logic, compliance officer clearance must be recorded.
