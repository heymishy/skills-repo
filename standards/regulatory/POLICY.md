# Regulatory Compliance Policy Floor

**Discipline:** regulatory
**lastReviewedBy:** Hamish
**lastReviewedDate:** 2026-04-11

These floors are binary. A delivery either meets them or it does not. No domain or squad override may relax these floors.

## Overview

These binary floors define the minimum delivery bar for regulatory compliance work. No domain, squad, or surface variant may relax these requirements.

## Outcomes

- Compliance obligations are identified before implementation so that gaps are not discovered during audit.
- Every change to a regulated system has an approval record so that the change is independently auditable.
- Compliance evidence exists for every mapped obligation so that auditors can verify adherence without accessing squad-internal systems.

## Requirements

- MUST document all applicable regulatory obligations before implementation begins so that compliance gaps are not discovered during post-delivery audit
- MUST obtain a change approval record before any change that alters the compliance posture of a regulated system is deployed so that the change is auditable and reversible
- MUST produce compliance evidence for each mapped obligation in a form that an independent auditor can verify without access to squad-internal systems

## Out of Scope for this standard

- Jurisdiction-specific regulatory interpretation
- Legal advice on regulatory applicability
- Compliance management tooling selection
