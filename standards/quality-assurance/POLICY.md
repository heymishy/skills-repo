# Quality Assurance Policy Floor

**Discipline:** quality-assurance
**lastReviewedBy:** Hamish
**lastReviewedDate:** 2026-04-10

These floors are binary. A delivery either meets them or it does not. No domain or squad override may relax these floors.

## Floor Requirements

- MUST have a test plan authored and reviewed before implementation begins
- MUST maintain the automated regression suite without reduction unless a decision log entry approves the removal
- MUST produce test evidence that allows independent verification of coverage before the PR is merged
